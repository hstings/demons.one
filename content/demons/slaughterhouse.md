## Biography
Slaughterhouse is an extreme demon created by IcEDCave. It is a sequel/remake of IcEDCaves impossible level "Slaughterhouse," aptly with the same name. It was initially **hack-verified** by spaceuk, with Doggie being the first victor. When spaceuk confessed to hacking, it was revealed that Doggie was the true verifier of the level.
