## Biography
Tidal Wave is an extreme demon created by OniLink. The level was verified by Zoink on September 9, 2023 after 49,534 attempts. The level is a beach/ocean themed wave level, which was praised at the time for it's unique decoration compared to most other top extremes at the time.
